Who We Are

Iron Craft Engineering Solutions has been established in May 2021 representing as manufacturing industry in plastic & pharmaceutical allied machineries having enough capacity of industrial structural fabrication as well as general mild steel & stainless steel fabrication as per design & drawings.

We deals in plastic allied machineries like conical colour mixers complete hot air stainless steel hopper dryers, Vaccum loader, spring loader, high speed mixers, rapid mixer dispenser, Anglomixers, Ovens, Shrink wrapping machine, Plastic waste grinders etc. We also deals in general machineries related to plastic industry like jumbo bag filling (packers) Screw Conveyors, Bucket Elevators, Clit belt Conveyors along with this we provide S.S. and M.S. jobs as per customers requirements like feeding hoppers, out centered feeding hoppers, extrusions rectangular tanks, M.S. & S.S. base structures etc.

We are young & energized engineers who can deliver the product or project within customer timeline with customer satisfied quality.

For Pharma industries we deals in pressure vessels, liquid filling vessels, double cone blender, sigma mixers, drum mixers, plough mixers, vibrators, agitators, reacting vessels, heat exchangers, condensers, storage tanks, drums, scoops etc.

We are having approx 7000 Sq.ft. area of factory with over headed crane facilities for light & heavy duty projects, Separate Machining workshop. Factory shed is having ample of water supply & enough electricity supply for to carry out light & heavy duty jobs on time as per customer requirement.
